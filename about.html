I am Flosha, an Acrobat on an endless search for beauty while trying to express love by words and in design.

You can find visual impressions of my work on my fsocial profile, which serves as my gallery. This page is focused on written content and can be seen as my "blog".

In the "Disentangler of Confusion" (german: Entflechter der Wirrnis), I try to summarise what I think and extract the essence of the thoughts by others to make it simple to understand. The goal of this project is, at some point, to summarise all of these findings again in an even more condensed form to serve as philosophical and practical guidance, even if just for myself; but I am sure that it will also serve others.

I write on my physical practices (Acrobatics and Coaching), aesthetical themes (art and intimacy) and spiritual/social matters (in form of samyayoga and its social work).

Since my youth I study Samyayoga, the philosophy of Vinoba that combines spiritual and social practice with an anarchist perspective and in this regard I created a website called the Sarvodaya Network that I plan to revive in the future.

Since 2014 I do acrobatics focused on Mixed Pair Hand to Hand and floor acro. In 2022 I became a student of Galina Sinyavskaya of SinY. I also learned from Lewie West (see his website I made for him) and a little bit from Aleksandr Kurennoi.

Since 2016 I am the creative director of Phoenix, an rpg project founded by me and friends of mine.

Currently I am training under the tutelage of Galina Sinyavskaya, I work on the AcroLib and try to lay foundations for a new acrobatic show group. Another project of mine is fsocial, the goal of which is to be a suckless way of doing social media platforming, how to do it remains to be clarified.

Enquiries at @floshin:matrix.org.
